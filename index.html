Avatar Hash

Generate unique avatars from any text.

Enter any text and enjoy:

Customizable, Simple and Beautiful

AvatarHash has 1 theme option, with 6 body parts, each part with 5 variations dynamically colored.

(More shapes and themes soon)

How to use?

1. Insert this script tag in your HTML:

<script src='https://unpkg.com/avatar-hash@latest/dist/avatar-hash.js'></script>

2. Generate an avatar from your text and render on your element:

<div class="avatar"></div>

<script>
new AvatarHash('my text').render(document.querySelector('.avatar'))
</script>
